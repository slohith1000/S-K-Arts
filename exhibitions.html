Exhibitions

Bengaluru Exhibition (2019 – 2023)

Chitra Santhe is one of Bengaluru’s most prestigious open-air art festivals, bringing together artists from across the country on a single vibrant platform.

The artist participated in Chitra Santhe over multiple years, showcasing original artworks to a diverse and enthusiastic audience.

The event provided direct interaction with art lovers, collectors, and critics, creating meaningful conversations around creativity and expression.

Each participation strengthened artistic confidence and public engagement, making Chitra Santhe a significant part of the Bengaluru exhibition journey.

The experience contributed greatly to artistic exposure and recognition.

Participating in exhibitions alongside fellow artists was a deeply enriching experience for my father, both personally and professionally.

The Bengaluru exhibitions allowed him to observe evolving art trends while staying rooted in his unique artistic voice.

Engaging with visitors and receiving feedback helped refine his approach to composition, color, and emotional depth.

The continuous participation over five years reflected dedication, discipline, and a strong commitment to artistic growth.

These experiences remain an important chapter in his artistic journey.

Mysuru Exhibition (2024)

The 2024 Mysuru art exhibition marked a significant milestone in the artist’s exhibition journey, held in a city renowned for its rich cultural and artistic heritage.

The exhibition presented a thoughtfully curated collection of artworks that reflected maturity in expression, refined techniques, and deeper emotional narratives.

For my father, exhibiting in Mysuru was a deeply meaningful experience, as the audience showed genuine appreciation and thoughtful engagement with his work.

Interactions with visitors, fellow artists, and art enthusiasts provided valuable feedback and encouragement, reinforcing his artistic vision.

This exhibition strengthened his confidence and remains an important chapter in his continuing artistic journey.
