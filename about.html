About the Artist

Shivakumara N is a contemporary artist whose work explores emotion, movement, and natural form. His paintings focus on expressive color, layered textures, and personal storytelling.

The artist has participated in multiple exhibitions and continues to experiment with modern techniques while preserving traditional values.
