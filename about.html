About the Artist

Shivakumara N, born on January 26, 1975, is a dedicated contemporary artist whose life has been deeply intertwined with art for nearly four decades. From a very young age, he discovered not just a talent for drawing and painting, but a purpose — to express emotions, stories, and human experiences through colors and forms. What began as a childhood passion slowly transformed into a lifelong journey of discipline, learning, and artistic exploration. Over the years, his work has evolved with time, yet his soul remains rooted in authenticity, simplicity, and emotional depth.

With close to 40 years of experience, Shivakumara N has devoted his entire life to the world of art. His practice spans across oil paintings, watercolors, pencil sketches, portraits, wall and interior paintings, street art, and large-scale visual works. Every piece he creates carries a part of his journey — the struggles, the patience, the observations of everyday life, and the quiet emotions that often go unnoticed. For him, art is not just a profession, but a way of living, a way of feeling, and a way of connecting with people. Through his work, he continues to inspire, preserve artistic traditions, and leave behind stories that speak directly to the heart.
